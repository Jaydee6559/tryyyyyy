About Sapientia University

Founded in 1959 by Dr. Sheilamae Bagasbas and Dr. Jamela Ann Rayos, Sapientia University began as an elementary school on the Bagasbas family estate. The name, Latin for "wisdom," was chosen to reflect the founders' belief that education should cultivate not just knowledge, but the wisdom and ethical judgment needed to serve the nation.

Our Mission

Our mission is to serve the nation by providing unparalleled access to quality education that cultivates expertise, ethical character, and a spirit of competitive excellence. Dedication to preparing students for successful and meaningful careers through rigorous academic endeavor, instilling in them the values of perseverance, honor, and outright devotion to their country.

Our Vision

To be the nation's leading institution of learning, distinguished by exceptional facilities, a world-class faculty, and a dynamic environment that develops determined and virtuous leaders. We envision a community of graduates who are equipped to address our country's most pressing challenges with ingenuity, honor, and unwavering devotion.

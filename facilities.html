University Facilities

Discover our state-of-the-art facilities designed to provide the best learning environment and student experience

Auditorium

Our 500-seat auditorium features cutting-edge audio-visual equipment, perfect for lectures, seminars, conferences, and cultural events. Equipped with professional lighting and sound systems.

500 Seats AV System Projection

Garden

A peaceful oasis in the heart of campus featuring indigenous plants, walking paths, and sitting areas. Perfect for studying, relaxing, or enjoying nature between classes.

Native Plants Seating Areas Walking Paths

Cafeteria

Our spacious cafeteria offers a variety of healthy and delicious meals, snacks, and beverages. Features multiple food stations, comfortable seating, and a welcoming atmosphere.

Multiple Cuisines Coffee Bar Free WiFi

Chapel

A serene space for prayer, meditation, and spiritual reflection. Open to all faiths and beliefs, providing a quiet retreat for students, faculty, and staff.

Meditation Music Peaceful

Computer Laboratory

State-of-the-art computer lab equipped with high-performance workstations, specialized software, and high-speed internet. Open for academic use and research projects.

High-end PCs High-speed Internet Printing Services

PE Center

Comprehensive sports and fitness facility featuring gym equipment, basketball courts, indoor track, and training areas. Supports both competitive sports and recreational activities.

Courts Gym Equipment Track

Library

A vast collection of academic resources, digital databases, and quiet study areas. Features group study rooms, computer access, and research assistance services.

100K+ Books Study Spaces Digital Resources

Admissions

Welcome to Sapientia University! We are excited to guide you through the admissions process. Whether you are a freshman or a transferee, please review the requirements below to prepare your application.

Freshman Requirements

  • Original High School Report Card (Form 138/SF9) in Grade 12
  • One copy of recent 1 x 1 ID picture
  • Original PSA Birth Certificate
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • Performance in the SU College Admission Test (SUCAT)

Transferee Requirements

  • Original Transfer Credentials or Honor Dismissal
  • Official Transcript of Records or True Copy of Grades from the last school attended
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • Course Descriptions
  • PSA Birth Certificate
  • One copy of recent 1 x 1 ID picture
